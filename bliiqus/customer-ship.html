SHIPPING POLICY

SHIPPING RATES

FREE GROUND SHIPPING: No more shipping fee for you. Free ground / home delivery within the continental United States.

EXPRESS SHIPPING: Looking to receive it sooner? You will only need to pay a flat rate of $20 per unit to receive a priority mail service. You will get your package within 2-3 business days!

INTERNATIONAL: Bliiq charges the exact USPS International Economy rate without markup.
EXCLUDES duties and taxes.

SHIPPING & DELIVERY TIMES

ORDER FULFILLMENT TIME: Bliiq ships your product in 1-2 business days of your order.

TRANSIT TIMES: USPS FIRST CLASS - ARRIVED IN 3 TO 7 BUSINESS DAYS
USPS PRIORITY - ARRIVED IN 2 TO 3 BUSINESS DAYS

EXCEPTIONS: Bliiq does not ship on weekends or public holidays and on some occasions may
take two business days to ship if there are any complications with your order. If your
product is on back order you will be notified before purchase (or possibly after sale if
unexpected sales volumes occur).

TRACKING: You will receive email confirmations upon order placement, and at time of shipment. Your
USPS shipment will begin tracking at the END OF DAY (or next morning) after your order
ships.

APO ADDRESS / MILITARY

Bliiq ships to Military APO addresses. When entering your shipping address on your order, enter your APO zip and our system will identify the region and fill it in for you. If you experience difficulty try changing the country from USA to the actual country the APO relates to.


Select shipping option "APO or PO Box delivery (USPS)" $19.99.


Shipping takes up to 14 days anywhere in the world. If any complications arise with placing an order to an APO / Military address please contact our customer service team.
